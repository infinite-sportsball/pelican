Red Sox KO Astros
It was all about winning for the Boston 1986 Red Sox -- and win they did.

There was nothing fancy in how they went about it, players said, they just prepared mentally and physically and then went out and played baseball.

"We did the little things right and we did the big things right," said starting pitcher Roger Clemens, who was named series MVP. "Now we're the league champions."

Boston 1986 defeated the Houston 1986 Astros by a score of 9-4 at Astrodome to take the series 1-0.

"Sometimes you get lucky and sometimes you make your own luck," said Boston 1986 manager Jim Smith. "We got lucky a few times, but mostly we did the right things by being prepared for each game and playing the game the way it's meant to be played."
